About the App

Chill Money is a calm and simple portfolio tracker that helps you keep an eye on your assets without the stress. Manage stocks, mutual funds, ETFs, crypto, savings, and more — all in one beautiful interface. Track your net worth over time with intuitive charts and enjoy a relaxed approach to personal finance.

Key Features

📊

Asset Overview

Organize your holdings across 22+ categories including stocks, ETFs, bonds, crypto, real estate, and more.

📈

Growth Charts

Visualize your total asset growth with charts spanning 7 days, 30 days, 90 days, or all time.

☁️

iCloud Sync

Automatically sync your data across all your Apple devices via iCloud. Easy migration when you switch phones.

🔒

Privacy First

All data stays on your device and iCloud. No external servers, no tracking, no analytics. Face ID supported.
