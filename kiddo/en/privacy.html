Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 23, 2025

UNDEFINED, K.K. ("we", "us", or "our") operates "Kiddo!" (the "App"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Account information (email address, authentication provider ID)
  • Children's nicknames
  • Task completion history, points, and reward records
  • Subscription purchase information
  • App usage data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing and operating the App's features
  • Managing user accounts
  • Managing subscriptions
  • Improving the App and service quality
  • Responding to inquiries

3. Information Sharing

We do not share personal information with third parties except:

  • With your consent
  • When required by law
  • To service providers necessary for operating the App

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In (authentication)
  • Sign in with Apple (authentication)
  • Supabase (database and authentication infrastructure)
  • RevenueCat (subscription management)

Please refer to each service's privacy policy on their respective websites.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is designed to be used by children under parental supervision. Children's accounts are created and managed by parents, and children do not directly input personal information. Children's nicknames are used only within the family.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. All data transmission uses SSL/TLS encryption.

7. Data Deletion

Users can delete their accounts from the Settings screen within the App. When an account is deleted, all associated data will be permanently removed.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App.
