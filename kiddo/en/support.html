❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q. How do I create an account?

A. After launching the app, tap "Sign in with Google" or "Sign in with Apple" to create your account.

Q. Is my child's information safe?

A. Yes. Your child's nickname is used only within your family and is not shared externally. All data is protected with SSL/TLS encryption.

Q. What's the difference between free and premium plans?

A. The free plan is limited to 2 tasks, 1 reward, and 1 child. The premium plan offers unlimited usage, custom interest rates, activity reports, and full history access.

Q. How do I delete my account?

A. Go to Settings within the app and select "Delete Account". All associated data will be permanently removed.

Q. What languages are supported?

A. The app supports Japanese and English. It automatically switches based on your device's language settings.

🔄 How to Cancel Your Subscription

To cancel the premium plan, follow these steps:

  1. Open the "Settings" app on your iPhone
  2. Tap your name (Apple ID) at the top
  3. Tap "Subscriptions"
  4. Select "Kiddo!"
  5. Tap "Cancel Subscription"

* After cancellation, you can continue using premium features until the end of your current billing period.
