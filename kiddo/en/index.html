About the App

Kiddo! is a family task management app that makes your child's efforts visible. Set household chores and daily habits as tasks — when your child completes them, they earn points! Points can be exchanged for rewards. Let's support your child's growth together as a family!

Features

📋

Task Management

Flexibly set daily, weekly, specific-day, or one-time tasks. Manage tasks that fit your child's routine.

🎁

Reward System

Exchange points earned from completing tasks for rewards. Boost your child's motivation.

🏦

Point Bank

Save points and earn interest. A fun way to learn about the value of money.

📊

Activity Reports

View your child's progress with charts. Share growth records with the family.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Multiple Children

Manage tasks and points for each sibling. Choose from cute characters.

🌍

Japanese & English

Supports Japanese and English. Perfect for families abroad.
